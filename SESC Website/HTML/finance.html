Sustainable Finance

The Sustainable Finance working group champions financial systems that prioritize long-term environmental and social well-being. It encourages investments in green technologies, sustainable agriculture, clean energy, and inclusive development. The group also works to build awareness around Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) criteria, supporting stakeholders to redirect capital toward sustainability goals. By engaging with banks, investors, and regulators, it aims to make sustainable finance a mainstream practice in global and local economies.

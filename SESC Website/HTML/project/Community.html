Community Tree Planting Initiative

Our Community Tree Planting program brings together local volunteers to restore urban green spaces by planting native tree species. This initiative not only beautifies our neighborhood but also creates vital habitats for wildlife, improves air quality, and helps combat climate change.

Why Tree Planting Matters

A single mature tree can absorb up to 48 pounds of carbon dioxide per year and release enough oxygen for two people. Our urban tree canopy is essential for:

  • Reducing urban heat island effect
  • Preventing soil erosion
  • Providing shade and cooling
  • Supporting biodiversity

How It Works

Every second Saturday of the month, we meet at different locations around town to:

  • Remove litter from public spaces
  • Properly dispose of waste and recyclables
  • Identify recurring problem areas for advocacy
  • Educate community members about waste reduction

Safety First

We provide all necessary equipment including gloves, grabbers, safety vests, and bags. All volunteers receive a brief safety orientation.

Water Conservation Campaign

Our Water Conservation Campaign educates the community about simple but impactful ways to reduce water waste at home, in businesses, and public spaces.

Why Conserve Water?

Despite Earth being 70% water, only 3% is freshwater and less than 1% is accessible for human use. Our region faces increasing drought risks due to climate change.

Our Programs

  • Home Water Audits: Free consultations to identify water waste
  • Rain Barrel Workshops: Learn to harvest rainwater
  • Xeriscaping Classes: Create beautiful drought-resistant gardens
  • School Education: Water conservation curriculum for kids

Simple Water-Saving Tips

  1. Turn off the tap while brushing teeth (saves 4 gallons/minute)
  2. Fix leaks promptly
  3. Install low-flow showerheads
  4. Only run full loads in dishwashers and washing machines
  5. Water plants early morning or evening to reduce evaporation

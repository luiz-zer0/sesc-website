Special Recycling Drop-Off Locations

These items can't go in curbside bins but we have solutions:

  • Electronics: Monthly e-waste collection
  • Batteries: Drop at library or city hall
  • Light Bulbs: Hardware store take-back
  • Textiles: Clothing donation bins


Our Impact

Since launching our initiative, we've:

  • Placed 120 new recycling bins in public spaces
  • Increased community recycling rate from 32% to 58%
  • Diverted over 40 tons from landfills annually
  • Trained 2,000+ residents in proper recycling
